# EventChain Meeting Templates

## Overview
This document provides standardized templates for various types of meetings conducted within the EventChain project. These templates ensure consistency, proper documentation, and effective meeting outcomes.

## General Meeting Guidelines

### Before the Meeting
- Send agenda 24 hours in advance
- Include pre-read materials if necessary
- Confirm attendees and their roles
- Set up meeting room/video conference
- Prepare necessary materials and tools

### During the Meeting
- Start and end on time
- Follow the agenda
- Encourage participation from all attendees
- Document decisions and action items
- Keep discussions focused and productive

### After the Meeting
- Share meeting notes within 24 hours
- Follow up on action items
- Schedule follow-up meetings if needed
- Update project tracking tools
